🎯 How to Use

  1. Launch the Application: Click the "Launch Infinity Gauntlet" button above
  2. Explore the Gauntlet: Use your mouse to rotate and zoom the 3D model
  3. Activate Stones: Click on individual stones to see their unique effects
  4. Toggle Stones: Use the UI panel to enable/disable specific stones
  5. Perform the Snap: Click the "SNAP" button for the full effect
  6. Customize Settings: Adjust audio volume, auto-rotation, and effect intensity
